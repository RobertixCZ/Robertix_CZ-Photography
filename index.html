About Me

I was always interested in photography, but of course I started as almost everyone these days. My first photos were taken with my mobile phone. They haven't been anything special, but I started to feel that I might be passionate for it. At the end of the year 2020 I started to think that mobile photography wasn't filling me as I wanted. So I started to learn about cameras and equipment necessary for everyday photography. And after just a half a year I had my first proper camera in my hands.

As the time passed I started to be interested not only in digital photography, but also in classic analog. And now I use 50/50 digital and analog cameras on my adventures to capture the best vibes out there.

what am i doing?

  • I focus on landscape and street photography anywhere I go.
  • I am shooting digital and analog photos (black and white most of the time).
  • In my free time, I try to travel around the Czech republic and other European countries.
  • I post my best photos from my adventures and journeys on my Instagram.
